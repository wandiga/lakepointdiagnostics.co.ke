The laboratory offers a wide range of tests which includes the following:

  • Clinical chemistry

  • Histopathology
  • Cytopathology
  • And a wide variety of molecular testing, including HIV PCR, Hepatitis B and C PCR , HPV, Mycobaterium tuberculosis (MTB PCR), and DNA paternity among others.

Sample collection

We pick samples from hospitals, clinics, laboratories and other facilities within a radius of 7 kilometers from the laboratory on a regular basis and not less than twice a day depending on the need. Arrangements have been made with specific courier services to transport samples from facilities situated outside 7 kilometers radius at the laboratory's cost.

Results

When ready, reports are likewise delivered to the requesting clinicians, doctors or facilities in sealed envelopes as and when they become ready. Emailing of reports to patients, and their doctors / clinicians or facilities from which test requests originated from are done to designated and approved email addresses that does not jeopardize patient reports confidentiality.
