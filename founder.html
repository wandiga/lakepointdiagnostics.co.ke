About the Founder

The mind (and heart) behind your care

Mr. Paul Okwach is a seasoned medical laboratory professional with over 30 years of experience, specializing in quality assurance and laboratory management. He began his career in 1990 as a trainee technologist at the University of Nairobi's Department of Veterinary Anatomy. In 1995, Mr. Okwach joined Aga Khan University Hospital, where he quickly rose to become the Technical Head of the Clinical Chemistry & Immunology department.

In 2002, Mr. Okwach co-founded Pathcare Kenya Limited as the Technical Manager, where he was instrumental in leading the lab to become the first in the region to achieve SANAS accreditation in 2004. After nearly a decade with Pathcare, he pursued further studies, earning a degree in Medical Laboratory Sciences from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) in 2014.

Mr. Okwach has held several key positions, including Quality Assurance Manager at Medanta Africare, Head of Customer Services at Pathologists Lancet Kenya, and a laboratory consultant for various institutions seeking international accreditation. His expertise also took him to Ghana, where he served as a consultant for Medlab Ghana Ltd.

Returning to Kenya, Mr. Okwach rejoined Pathcare in 2018 as the Technical Manager for its Mombasa branch and later became the Regional Technical Manager for Western Kenya. His focus on quality and technical excellence has always been his guiding principle, a commitment that eventually led him to establish Lakepoint Diagnostic Laboratories in 2024.

Throughout his career, Mr. Okwach has been a passionate advocate for laboratory accreditation and quality standards, contributing significantly to KENAS accreditation campaigns and serving on its first steering committee. At Lakepoint Diagnostic Laboratories, he promises to uphold the highest standards of quality testing for all clients, ensuring reliable and accurate diagnostic services.
